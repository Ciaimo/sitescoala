Frequently Asked Questions

What is Apitoxin?

Apitoxin, commonly known as bee venom, is a natural substance produced by honeybees. It has various therapeutic properties and is used in skincare products.

How is Apitoxin collected?

Apitoxin is collected using special glass or plastic sheets that encourage bees to sting the surface. The venom is then carefully scraped off and purified.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all our products are cruelty-free. We prioritize ethical and sustainable practices in our beekeeping and product manufacturing processes.
