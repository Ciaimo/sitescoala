Featured Product

Apitoxin

Apitoxin, commonly known as bee venom, is a natural substance produced by honeybees. It is a complex mixture of enzymes, peptides, and proteins with various therapeutic properties. Known for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, apitoxin is used in skincare products to stimulate collagen production, promote skin regeneration, and reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
